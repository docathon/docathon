Docathon

Hosts

While it's totally fine to participate in the Docathon remotely (all you need to do is contribute to documentation!), some will gather at host sites for tutorials, hacking sessions on a specific project, or simply to share a good time amongst documentation-lovers!

Here is a list of organizations / groups that are holding docathon parties during the week. You are welcome to host your own Docathon, click here. If you'd like join one of these locations, check out the details of the specific events. Don't forget to register if necessary!

  • BIDS, UC Berkeley, CA - Signup / Agenda
  • Digital Fellows (GCDI), The Graduate Center, NY - Signup / Agenda
  • eScience Institute, UW Seattle, WA - Signup / Agenda

If you'd like to work remotely, but want to be part of a greater community, we have a slack channel for communicating before and during the week. You can join here, or go directly to https://docathon.slack.com if you already have a login.

Sponsors

Thanks to all of the organizations that have supported the docathon, who have given funding, space, and occasionally pizza + coffee for us to organize.
